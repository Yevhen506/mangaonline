Aizawa-san Zoushoku

相沢さん増殖, Aizawasan Zoushoku

Mizutani Souta is confessed to by the school's famous frail beauty Aizawa Shino. Then came another one of her! And another! And another! Each with different attitudes, but all of them are Aizawa Shino herself. What will this sort-of harem love story come to!? Another tsukkomi-all-you-can offering from the creator of Fujimura-kun Mates!
