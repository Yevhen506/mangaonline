All Hail Empress Her Majesty

拜见女皇陛下

Ming is a Year 5 primary school student who had a dull and meaningless school life in the worst class of the worst school in a declining heavy industry city. However one day his deskmate Hua became the class president and declared herself Empress. The class now goes on a journey to fight against the old order made by adults.
