The Almier

The Almier Nation was created by the world nations to contain and monitor individuals with supernatural abilities. However the Almier believe they can do more than just monitor extra-ordinary humans, they believe they can also help to achieve world peace and are trying to convince the world of this. At the same time, a small group of supernatural individuals who do not wish to be monitored or contained by the Almier set out to make their displeasure known.
