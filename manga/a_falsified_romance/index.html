A Falsified Romance

罪と罰 (落合尚之); A Falsified Romance; Crime and Punishment (OCHIAI Naoyuki)

Miroku Tachi was a promising student until he dropped out of college. Now, after months spent as a NEET (Not in Employment, Education or Training), he has hatched a plan to help his sister, himself, and the whole world around him ... by murdering someone. Based on Fyodor Dostoyevsky's classic novel CRIME AND PUNISHMENT, but set in modern day Japan.
