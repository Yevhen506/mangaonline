Across The Dangerous Ocean

深海危情

uer Le saved a mystery man from the deep sea, but she would never have thought that she would be dragged into a whirlpool of conspiracy... Best friend who went missing, dangerous investigator job, well-planned schemes, the bossy yet affectionate heir of the financial group, the quiet and gentle bodyguard, the proud gang childe, the mystery detective... It’s a story about trust and betrayal, conspiracy and truth, growth and love.
