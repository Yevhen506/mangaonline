Inaba of the Moon & Inaba of the Earth

Touhou - Inaba of the Moon or Inaba of the Earth; Touhou Bougetsushou - Tsuki no Inaba to Chijou no Inaba; 東方儚月抄 ～ 月のイナバと地上の因幡
